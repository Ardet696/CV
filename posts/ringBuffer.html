Writing a lock-free ring buffer for audio

Moving from theory to practice, fast & efficient ring buffer.

The problem

An audio callback runs on a real-time thread and must never block. The moment you reach for a mutex, you risk priority inversion and glitches. A single-producer / single-consumer ring buffer sidesteps the problem entirely.

The decode thread writes, the audio thread reads, and neither ever waits on the other.
